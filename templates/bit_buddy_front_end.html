WELCOME!

Hi, new here? Welcome!
You have stumbled across your soon to be most used website for all your food needs.
We the Heartfelt Hacktivists understand the struggles that have come with the cost of living crisis including those who may be suffering with food insecurity.
The usual household items are forever inflating in price with 90% of countries experiencing food inflation.
Well lucky for you we are here to help!
Here at BiteBuddy we will connect you with your local foodhubs, food pantrys and other services providing food at little to no cost!
That's right FREE FOOD!!
Not only that, we also provide you with recipe ideas to use those new free supplies you just obtbained as well as the food within your own pantry.

Our goal here at BiteBudy is to reduce the wastage of food Navigating the Intersection of Taste and Convenience Welcome to Bitebuddy, a dynamic and innovative website that brings together the world of culinary exploration and sustainable choices, all while keeping our preferences in mind. The heart of this hackathon project lies behind Bitebuddy's goal: to revolutionize how we discover and engage with food hubs, recipes, and events.
